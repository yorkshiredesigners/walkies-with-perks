"Walkies With Perks" gives your dog the type of walks which help them to truly thrive! Our average walk can cover between 2–4 miles!

"Do you need a helping hand in giving your dog the exercise and mental stimulation it needs? Whatever your circumstances we are here to help with 'Personalised dog walking to suit you & your dogs needs.'"

Why do we say Personalised dog walking? Isn't a dog walk simply a dog walk? Our answer to that is 'NO'! Because the needs of each dog can vary as well as the needs of each owner. We take pride in getting to know what you & your dog truly require and need, to make both owner & dog happy with our dog walking services.

Walking is an essential part of a dogs daily life! It provides vital exposure, keeps them physically fit, gives them their 'natural and vital way' to have a physical and mental outlet to stimulate their minds. Walks give dogs a chance to explore, grow in confidence, providing opportunities to be balanced around other dogs whilst also helping them to interact with people safely. All of this can greatly reduce behaviour problems if done correctly! Walks should not be chaotic, they need to be calm with rules, boundaries and plenty of outlets when needed.

If your dog is regularly chewing or destroying things in your home, or perhaps they seem to bark at everything and anything. This might be because they are in need of more mental and physical stimulation. They could have pent-up frustration within them that needs to be addressed. Daily structured walks which suit their individual needs can often remove these issues for some dogs.

Wide Variety of Walks in Different Locations!!

Whether we walk your dog once a week or 5 times a week, they will visit a wide variety of walking locations with us. Giving varied, good quality mental and physical stimulation is something we feel very passionate about at Walkies With Perks because it is so beneficial for dogs!

We love giving them walks which enables them to go home feeling tired and fully satisfied. One of the great ways to achieve this is by taking them to lots of different locations in the local area. Many other benefits come from this including confidence building.

Your dog will never get bored on Walkies With Perks!

All our walks include;

  • Plenty of Physical & Mental Stimulation to tire each dog out
  • Good 'doggy manners' encouraged
  • Good 'recall' worked on safely
  • Good Socialisation with dogs & people
  • Good 'Lead' work encouraged

Group Walks

A Group Walk in public areas & public fields we will take between 2–4 dogs.

A Group Walk on enclosed fields we will take between 3–8 dogs.

We only introduce dogs to a group walk after we have become sufficiently acquainted with them. And we only introduce dogs who are able to be around other dogs whilst respecting another dogs boundaries and/or listen to human leadership.

Please Note: Dogs who like other dogs but don't respond to human leadership or don't respect another dogs boundaries 'need extra help or training' before being on group walks. We can provide this as a dog training service. Please enquire if you'd like more information.

FREE OF CHARGE PERKS; We will often add an additional 15 minutes to a walk if your dog requires the extra time or if we're simply having a great time. Our passion for dog walking involves tiring each dog out mentally and physically (age & health permitting). Some dogs naturally have extra energy to burn. For these dogs to truly benefit from a walk they need to get tired.

Our job satisfaction is giving the dog the best we can in the time we have so if we have the time and they need that extra 15 minutes we give it for no extra charge.

Booking Process;

We offer a FREE no-obligation meet and greet before any bookings are made.

Before we take our first steps together we think its very important and beneficial to your dog(s), for us to gain as much information as possible about them from you. If you decide to make a booking we will collect your dog(s) from your home and either walk them from there into familiar surroundings, exploring local parks, fields and tracks or we also have the option of transport to take your pooch to different walk locations, adding to their stimulation and adventure.

Our aim is to build a trusting bond with you and your dog(s) so that when they see us they know they are going to have a super fun and fulfilling time!

Free photos and videos can be taken during the time we spend together and shared with you.

"Do you need a helping hand in giving your dog the training and/or exercise it needs to be happy and to thrive?"

Walkies With Perks provide Dog Training & Dog Walking Services with no judgements.

Our Dog Training Services not only teaches you how to lead your dog correctly but also enables you to understand your dogs psychology and mental state so that you can fix unwanted behaviours.

Dogs need to be walked everyday for at least 45 minutes. Dogs are not happy & fulfilled when they don't have varied, daily walks. They can become mentally distressed and some can become very difficult to manage. For dogs who do get the right amount of daily exercise and are still displaying unwanted behaviours then the owners need 'Dog Training'. On the same note, if you'd love to be able to walk your dog everyday but have reached a brick wall due to not being able to control their behaviour, please get in touch to discuss how we can help you and your dog.

Ultimately though, its important all dog owners accept that even though its called 'Dog Training', in reality its dog owners that need training. If dog owners are not willing to make changes and to do their best at practicing the techniques you are taught, then your dog will go back to its old ways!

What is NeMo® Dog Training?

NeMo® Dog Training is a world renowned training system! Jemma, the founder of Walkies With Perks has been Mentored and Accredited in the NeMo® Training System and undergone extensive training to meet the highest levels of training ethics coupled with a genuine love for dogs.

Mother nature knows best! NeMo® dog training imitates dog to dog interactions by creating leadership and correcting any imbalances through Negative Motivation (reinforcement) and Positive Physical Praise at the right time, combined with the correct exercise which creates a happy, neutral, submissive mental state; the happiest, most stable mental state for a dog to be in with its human owner. It brings the dog into 'Pack Drive', into a follower state of mind, making the dog feel safe, growing their self-confidence and self-control whilst establishing true engagement with trust and respect between dog and handler. Please note, using correct negative motivation/reinforcement does NOT mean bullying the dog into a supressed state. Its essential to get proper training before attempting any of the mentioned methods.
